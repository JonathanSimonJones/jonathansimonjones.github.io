262

May 23rd, 2014

Task - Create a flash game
Software used - Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Flash Professional
Languages used - ActionScript 2.0
Description -
262 is a 2D shooter based around defending your ship from incoming aliens who are trying to come over the barricade to attack the ship. The enemies approach from the right side of the screen and stop to attack the barricade. To stop the aliens the player has a turret which has a limited degree of motion. The objective of the game is to try and stop as many enemies as you can!

The play the game follow this link: 262 Game
(WARNING game audio starts immediately and is quite loud!)
