Stitch Scene

Jul 24th, 2014

Overview

A 3D scene which loads modells and uses the Playstation 2’s vector unit to simulate the movement of water.

Description

This programs main purpose was to show effective use of the Playstation 2 vector unit. In the video above you can see the waves move which is being calculated on the vector units. The scene also demonstates use of lighting effects, loading of 3D models and the use of a Skybox.
